McDonald's

International Food Items

New Zealand

Many McDonald's menu items in New Zealand are similar to those in Australia. Some unique items include the Kiwiburger, a beef patty with McMuffin egg, tomato, lettuce, cheese, onion, beetroot, ketchup, and mustard

Malaysia

Among the local bestseller in the Malaysian market includes the Ayam Goreng McD, originally branded as McDonald's Fried Chicken, introduced in March 1986. The product's name was changed to Chicken McCrispy in 2000 and again to Ayam Goreng McD in 2002 to complement the country's culture. There are two variants of the fried chicken: Regular and Spicy.

The Phillipines

Example of Philippine McDonald's menu consist of Chicken Fillet Ala King served with fries, cola and steamed rice.

Japan

Japan has the Idaho Burger, which includes bacon, beef, onion, cheese and pepper sauce.
